Questions? Comments? Troubleshooting?

If you have any feedback for us, or you are having any troube with KindleFolder, please email us at support@kindlefolder.us

Troubleshooting:

  • Documents not sending: Did you add unicorns@kindlefolder.us to your Kindle whitelist? Visit the about page to see how.
  • Documents not sending: Have you recently changed your kindle email address? If so, unsubscribe, then sign up again.
  • Service is slow: Please be aware that the KindleFolder service runs off of Amazon Kindle's Personal Document Service, which can be slow at times.
